Welcome To

Infomax College of IT and Management

Situated within the heart of Pokhara, Infomax College of IT & Management is one of the pioneers of IT and Management education in and around Pokhara valley.

Established by IT Experts, when the IT industry was within the starting organization and there was no Market-oriented Management program.

As an academic institution, we are committed to cultivating development and enterprise where we energize our students to think critically, embrace creativity, and pursue their passions by ensuring our students are well-arranged for the professional world.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, cutting-edge research facilities, and open and spacious buildings secured with lush greenery, where you'll stop, breathe within the new discussion, and submerge yourself in the tranquility of the environment.
